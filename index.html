CXSTOMER 5th Anniversary - Regional Meet

LUZON TAKEOVER

Join us for a thoughtfully curated evening of connection, conversation, and celebration as Cxstomer marks its 5th year anniversary!

View Details

Event details

This marks the 5th year of Cxstomer—a milestone made meaningful by the people behind it. Join us in Luzon for an intimate gathering designed to bring the community together through conversation, connection, and shared moments.

An evening awaits where guests can enjoy a thoughtfully curated dining experience, meaningful exchanges, and a relaxed atmosphere that celebrates how far we've come—together.

FAQs

Is parking available at the venue?

Yes, parking will be available for guests at the venue.

Who can I contact for questions?

For any inquiries, you may reach out via Slack to Blad, KM, HEs, or Pearl.

How do I get to the venue?

You can find the exact location and directions here: https://maps.app.goo.gl/XkgDe8V6GSvE9nJg8. We recommend checking the route in advance and allowing extra travel time on the day of the event.

Will there be room arrangements for overnight stays?

Room assignments and bed allocations will be shared soon. We aim to provide each guest with their own bed, though some room sharing may be required.

Will there be transportation provided?

Yes, a free van transfer will be available on April 18, 2026 going to the event, and on April 19, 2026 for the return trip. More details will be shared soon.

What if my schedule conflicts with the event?

For concerns regarding schedules, leaves, or PTO, please coordinate with Miss Diana Suarez or your respective Team Leads.

We're saving a seat for you.

Questions? Reach out on Slack: #cxstomer-events
